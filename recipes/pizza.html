Pizza

Description

Pizza is a globally loved Italian dish made with a round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with a variety of ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese, vegetables, meats, and herbs. Baked to perfection in an oven, pizza is known for its deliciously crispy crust, gooey melted cheese, and endless customization possibilities. Whether it’s a classic Margherita or a loaded veggie or meat lover’s pizza, this dish is a perfect blend of taste, texture, and comfort that’s enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredient

For Chole
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 to 1.5 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., capsicum, onion, tomato, mushrooms, olives, jalapeños, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil. Knead into a smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1–1.5 hours or until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F).
  5. Punch down the dough and roll it into a circular base on a floured surface.
  6. Place the rolled-out dough on a baking tray or pizza pan.
  7. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  8. Add your favorite toppings and sprinkle grated mozzarella cheese on top.
  9. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes for extra flavor.
  10. Bake the pizza for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese has melted.
  11. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot!
